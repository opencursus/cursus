# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant**, version 2.1.

The full text is available at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project (issues, pull requests, discussions),
you agree to abide by its terms.

## Reporting

Reports of violations should be sent to:

> **conduct@opencursus.app**

Reports are reviewed by the maintainers. We will respect confidentiality
and aim to acknowledge within **3 working days**.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, commits,
code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to
this Code of Conduct, and may ban temporarily or permanently any
contributor for behaviours they deem inappropriate, threatening, or
harmful.
